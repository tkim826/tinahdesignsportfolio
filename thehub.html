The Hub: An App to Connect Communities

Problem: New residents often lack a connection to their neighborhood, increasing the likelihood of moving again, and weakening communities further.

Solution: By educating users about the history of their neighborhood and local businesses through The Hub, residents will have a better understanding and connection to their community. It would also help promote local business to lessen the gap between newer transplants and established neighbors.

Tools: Sketch, InVision, Paper, Pen, Adobe Illustrator
