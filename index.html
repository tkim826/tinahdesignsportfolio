About Me

Hi there! I'm a UX Designer with expertise in: product design and development, branding, qualitative research, quantitative research, and project management. For the past 14 years, I have designed with the end user in mind. Who is my customer? What are his/her needs? How can I improve the product and execute it in the most efficient way possible? These are the questions that I have asked myself on a daily basis.
