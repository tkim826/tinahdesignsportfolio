About Me

Hi there! There has never been a doubt that I would be a designer. I pursued my career at Otis College of Art & Design and majored in Fashion Design.For the past 13 years, I have designed with the end user in mind. Who is my customer? What are his/her needs? How can I improve the product and execute it in the most efficient way possible? These are the questions that I asked myself on a daily basis.

Recently, my passion for design has led me to branch into UX Design. At first glance, they may seem really different, but they are actually very similar. Researching needs and current trends, building personas, moodboards, designing, prototyping, reiterating prototypes, and finally going into production- these are the steps involved in the design process for both fields. Since technology is always changing and evolving, it is exciting to continue this journey into UX design.
