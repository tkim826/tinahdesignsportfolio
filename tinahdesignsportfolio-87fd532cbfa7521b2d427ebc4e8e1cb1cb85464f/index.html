About Me

Hi there! There has never been a doubt that I would be a designer. I pursued my career at Otis College of Art & Design and majored in Fashion Design. For the past 13 years, I have designed with the end user in mind. Who is my customer? What are his/her needs? How can I improve the product and execute it in the most efficient way possible? These are the questions that I asked myself on a daily basis.

Recently, I came to the realization that it was not just fashion that I loved, but the design process as a whole.

  • Researching current needs and trends, finding opportunities, building personas, ideating, designing, prototyping, testing, iterating until the product is able to go to production...

My love for this process led me to look for other outlets of design- which is how I ended up in UX Design. I am excited to continue my journey in UX, as I evolve and continue to learn with the constantly changing technology...
